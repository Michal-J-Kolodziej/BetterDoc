# Clarification Question Bank

Use these only when answers materially change scope, architecture, or sequencing.
Pick the smallest subset needed.

## Product Direction

1. Who is the primary user segment?
2. What is the single most important user outcome?
3. Which one user action defines success in MVP?

## Platform and Delivery Constraints

1. Which platform is required for MVP: web, iOS, Android, or cross-platform?
2. Is there a delivery deadline or fixed budget boundary?
3. Must the app support multiple tenants/workspaces from day one?

## Data and Integrations

1. Which external systems are mandatory for launch?
2. Is offline support required for core workflows?
3. Are imports/exports required (CSV, PDF, API)?

## Security and Compliance

1. Is sensitive personal or health/financial data stored?
2. Are there compliance requirements (SOC 2, HIPAA, GDPR)?
3. Are role-based permissions required in MVP?

## Business Model and Growth

1. Is monetization needed in MVP (subscription, one-time, free)?
2. Is self-serve onboarding required, or can onboarding be manual first?
3. Is analytics instrumentation required before launch?
